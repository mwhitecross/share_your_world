Welcome to your hockey journey

Why learn to play hockey?

Hockey is a great way to connect with others, lots of people love the sport and connect with people due to that. It is a sport that gives good exercise and great ways to meet new people.

Isn't it hard?

Yes at first hockey might seem hard to learn and get into. Even so as soon as you are able to play well you will see that it is fun!

Where do I start?

You can start with many different skill level leagues, many start with a beginner league but if you are up to the challenge you can join a recreational league.
